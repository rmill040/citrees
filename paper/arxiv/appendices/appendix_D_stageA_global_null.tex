\section{Proof of Proposition~\ref{prop:stageA-global-null}}
\label{app:proof-stageA-global}

\paragraph{Claim.} Proposition~\ref{prop:stageA-global-null}.
\paragraph{Assumptions used.} A0.1--A0.5 (\cref{app:assumptions}).

Stage~A rejects the global null only if
$\min_{j \in F_t} p_{t,j} \le \alpha_{\mathrm{sel}}/m_t$. Under the global null,
each $p_{t,j}$ is super-uniform, and the union bound gives
%
\begin{equation}
  \PP\!\left(\min_{j \in F_t} p_{t,j} \le \alpha_{\mathrm{sel}}/m_t\right)
  \le \sum_{j \in F_t} \PP(p_{t,j} \le \alpha_{\mathrm{sel}}/m_t)
  \le m_t \cdot \frac{\alpha_{\mathrm{sel}}}{m_t}
  = \alpha_{\mathrm{sel}}.
\end{equation}
