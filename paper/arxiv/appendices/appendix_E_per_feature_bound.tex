\section{Proof of Proposition~\ref{prop:per-feature-bound}}
\label{app:proof-per-feature}

\paragraph{Claim.} Proposition~\ref{prop:per-feature-bound}.
\paragraph{Assumptions used.} A0.1--A0.5 (\cref{app:assumptions}).

If node $t$ splits on feature $j$, then Stage~A must have rejected for $j$ at the
Bonferroni threshold, hence $p_{t,j} \le \alpha_{\mathrm{sel}}/m_t$. Therefore,
%
\begin{equation}
  \PP(\text{node $t$ splits on $j$})
  \le \PP(p_{t,j} \le \alpha_{\mathrm{sel}}/m_t)
  \le \alpha_{\mathrm{sel}}/m_t
\end{equation}
%
by super-uniformity of $p_{t,j}$ under the null.
