\section{Conclusion}
\label{sec:conclusion}

We studied conditional inference trees and forests that use Monte Carlo
permutation tests as the inferential backbone for Stage~A variable selection.
This paper emphasizes finite-sample, fixed-node (especially root) guarantees
in fixed-$B$ mode and a reproducible benchmark protocol for evaluating feature
rankings. We also discuss practical optimization strategies for scaling
permutation-based variable selection in trees and forests. For reproducibility,
we provide a reference implementation.
